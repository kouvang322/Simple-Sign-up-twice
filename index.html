About Twice

Twice is a K-pop girl group under the company JYP-Entertainment in South Korea. Their group consists of five korean members, three japanese members, and one taiwanese member, 9 in total.

Twice was formed under the television program "Sixteen", and debuted in 2015. Since their debut, they have become one of the most accomplished girl-group in the history of K-pop, becoming the nation's girl-group.

They have had breakthrough successes throughout their career and even found international fame ion countries, such as Japan, Thailand, Singapore, Philippines. Recently they have started to promote more in the US markets.

Twice's Achievements

  • First K-Pop girl group to sell 100,000 online concert tickets
  • First K-Pop girl group to surpass 200,000 sales in one day
  • First K-Pop girl group to surpass 5 million sales
  • First K-Pop girl group to achieve 100 PAKs(Perfect All-Kills)
  • First K-Pop girl group to hold a Japan dome tour
  • First K-Pop girl group to surpass 1 billion streams
  • First K-Pop girl group to debut at #1 on both Billboard World charts
  • First K-Pop girl group to receive Gaon platinum certification
  • First K-Pop girl group to hit 5 million followers on Twitter
  • First K-Pop girl group to win SOTY(Song of the Year) three years in a row
